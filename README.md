# SMALL KINE SURF

Arcade style surfing game inspired by the old school NES T&C  surf game built using Godot 4.5.

Play at https://bbtinkerer.itch.io/small-kine-surf

## Description

Get barreled or air, its up to you. Earn points for staying in the tube and awarded big bonuses the longer you are barreled and successfully exit. To land the jumps, you need to come down at the  mirror angle you took off from.  You get a free ride by scoring 5,000 points per ride as indicated by the golden heart.


### Controls

- A/D to move left/right from the surfer's point of view.
- Space or Enter  to slow down
- PgUp/PgDown cycle through music
- M to mute
